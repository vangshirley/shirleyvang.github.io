CITY DATING APP


OUTLINE:
City Dating App currently has 2 dating apps for Portland, and Seattle.
They will be expanding in the coming months and need new localized branding/logos.
This includes logos, color schemes, screenshots, etc. for each new city.

GOALS AND OBJECTIVES (Deliverables)
Design cohesive logos for dating apps that users will easily be able to identify, and is aligned with the brand/company.

EXPANSION PLAN:
Conceive, design, and develop surveys and experiments to engage our users in meaninful feedback.
Online research
Narrow down commonality from all search.

ROLE: UX Designer

Individual project reporting to Founder weekly on designs, and user feedback.

TIMELINE:
9 weeks (4 weeks design, 4 weeks user surveying, 1 week analysis, and iteration.)


LOGO ICON FOR SACRAMENTO, CA


Logos created for 9 different cities.

CHALLENGE: creative block for design, and capturing essence of Sacramento to draw enough attention for clicks.

RIGHT: TOWER BRIDGE
